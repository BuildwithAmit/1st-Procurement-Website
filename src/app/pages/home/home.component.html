Innovators Build Things

They build them using various materials and processes.

Materials are the core elements of engineering, a source of differentiation in any product or process.

In the process of developing a new product or process for material selection and procurement, innovators waste 60-70% of their product development time, money and resources using ‘ trial and error’ methodology.

In order to test new attributes and property sets for their products, innovators test approximately five to six raw materials for each part or process and fifteen to twenty raw materials for each product before finalizing the raw materials.
