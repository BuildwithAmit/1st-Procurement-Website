Our story

We firmly think that every great company concept stems from the founder's own pain points and problems. We understand the difficulties that hardware product developers face during the product development process because we have worked with numerous startups and individual innovators in developing their products.

We talked with a wide range of hardware product development researchers and innovators from many disciplines and sectors. We recognized that a fundamental barrier in the innovation process is a lack of access to the necessary materials and resources, which can lead to time and resource waste, increased expenses, and a longer time to market.

To address this, we created a platform that would assist innovators in discovering and sourcing raw materials for research and specialized production.

Vision

We aspire to build an ecosystem to assist innovators and researchers in developing hardware products.

Mission

Our mission is to assist innovators and researchers in achieving their hardware product development goals by leveraging data intelligence to inform raw material selection and procurement so that hardware development can be optimized for maximum efficiency.
