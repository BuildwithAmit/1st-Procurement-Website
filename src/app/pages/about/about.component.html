Our story

we strongly believe that every successful startup idea is born from the pain points and challenges that entrepreneurs have personally experienced. Having worked with numerous startups and individual innovators in developing their products, we understand the struggles that hardware product developers face during the product development process. To tackle these pain points head-on, we decided to delve deeper into the product development process to uncover the root causes of these challenges.

We interacted with numerous hardware product development researchers and innovators across different domains and sectors. We got to know that while developing new products, these innovators face numerous challenges, such as material selection and procurement, testing and validation, and ultimately, manufacturing.

One of the most significant challenges they face is discovering the right raw materials for their product and sourcing them. Materials are the core elements of engineering, and they are a source of differentiation in any product or process.

We realized that the lack of access to the right materials and resources is a major bottleneck in the innovation process. It leads to wastage of time and resources, higher costs, and longer time to market. Innovators and researchers are unable to focus on their research, as they are bogged down by admin work.

However, we saw an opportunity to make a difference. We decided to come up with a platform that would make it easier for innovators to discover and source raw materials for research and specialty production. The platform would serve as a one-stop-shop for innovators, providing access to a wide range of resources for material discovery and sourcing

Vision

We aspire to build an ecosystem to assist innovators and researchers in developing hardware products.

Mission

Our mission is to assist innovators and researchers in achieving their hardware product development goals by leveraging data intelligence to inform raw material selection and procurement so that hardware development can be optimized for maximum efficiency.
